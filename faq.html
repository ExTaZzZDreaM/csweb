What is VAC?

VAC stands for Valve Anti-Cheat, an automated system designed to detect cheats installed on users' computers. If a user connects to a VAC secure server from a computer that has cheats installed, the VAC system will prevent the user from playing that game on VAC secure servers in the future.

What is Trust Factor in CSGO?

At the moment, the developers do not provide a list of factors used in the Trust Factor matchmaking system. The developers don't want players to worry about any particular activity while playing CS:GO or other games on Steam. In addition, Trust Factor matchmaking will be constantly updated and any list of factors will become outdated very quickly. In general, the Trust Factor is determined by looking at the user's past experience on CS:GO and on Steam to ensure that the system is as accurate as possible.

What is the competitive rank system in CS:GO?

Similar to the Elo rating in chess, CS:GO Competitive Skill Groups predict how well players will play against other players. Two equally qualified teams should, on average, win an equal number of matches when competing against each other.
